Our Story

The Chair By
the Lake.

Bogey's Barber Shop on the Lakeside is the Mendota chair for men who want their hair done right. Located on Lakewood Drive, we're a community-focused shop known for clean fades, sharp beard work, and a straightforward walk-in friendly atmosphere.

From a classic taper to a full hot-towel shave, we take the time the cut deserves. Bring the kids, bring your dad, bring your buddy from the next fairway over.
